1. General Information

Oral-History.Digital ist eine Erschließungs- und Recherche-Plattform für wissenschaftliche Sammlungen von audiovisuell aufgezeichneten Interviews. Sie unterstützt sammelnde Institutionen und Forschungsprojekte (im Folgenden: Archivinhaber*innen) bei der Archivierung, Erschließung und Bereitstellung ihrer Interview-Archive.
Oral-History.Digital is a digital information infrastructure for scholarly collections of audiovisual narrative interviews. It supports collecting institutions and research projects (in the following: archive owners) in archiving, curating and presenting their interviews. Furthermore, it provides researchers with a portal for cross-collection research, annotation, and analysis.
Oral-History.Digital is operated by the University Library of the FU Berlin. The respective archive owners are responsible for the content of the individual archives and for the activation of users for these archives.

4. Application for Use 

To use Oral-History.Digital and the archives included there, you must apply for use by filling out and submitting the registration form. For data protection reasons, you will be asked to confirm your registration by e-mail. You are then registered as a user of Oral-History.Digital.

You can then request activation for the archives included in Oral-History.Digital. After successful verification by the archive owner, you will be able to access the content with your Oral-History.Digital login (e-mail address and password). The access data must be treated confidentially and is not to be disclosed to third parties. In case of misuse, the FU Berlin reserves the right to withdraw the permission of use and, if necessary, to initiate further legal action.

5. Permitted Use and Citation

Oral-History.Digital and the archives included may only be used within the scope of the research purpose and in the manner permitted by the respective archive.

In order to reproduce content, publish it, present it in public or pass it on to third parties, separate permission must be obtained from the archive owners.

In the case of citations in publications and presentations, the source must be indicated. Please note the citation method given for each interview, for example with regard to the abbreviation of surnames.
